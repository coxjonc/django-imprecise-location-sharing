Share (but don't overshare) your location

This app lets you easily keep track of what city your friends are in. Weekly email updates keep you informed on people's location changes. Unlike other location-sharing apps, it doesn't store or publish your precise location.

It's easy to lose track of people when you move to different cities. But with this app, I think you'll find you cross paths with old friends more often than you'd expect.
